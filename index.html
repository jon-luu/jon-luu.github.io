Problem Statement

Many University of South Carolina students forget their Carolina Card, so creating a digital version with tap and Apple Wallet features would make campus access and payments more convenient and secure.

Affinity Diagram

The affinity diagram highlights common student frustrations with physical Carolina Cards, such as losing or damaging them, carrying multiple cards, and facing limited scanner compatibility. It suggests creating a secure, all-in-one digital campus access app with features like NFC tap entry, QR code backup, and biometric authentication.

Sketches

These sketches outline the design flow of the One USC App, showing the login screen, student dashboard, Face ID verification, and final digital card access. They illustrate secure login and convenient biometric authentication for campus entry and payments.
